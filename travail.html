FRENCH WITH VERICHIMA INTENSIVE LEARNING

(French for everyday use)

French is arguably one of the most pleasant languages to the ear but it can also sound complex, expansive and rich. With its complexities, you can still arrive at an advance level even as a beginner currently. French with ‘Verichima’has been established to train and educate Pupils, Students, Professionals, Clergies, Business owners and Company Managers on French language and Culture online.


OUR SERVICES


BEGINNER

This is the most basic level that would highlight your first knowledge of the French Language. You are expected to interact in the simplest way possible by learning to speak about your self and your immediate environment.

INTERMEDIATE

This is targeted at French Language learners with interest in language and culture. At this stage, it is assumed that you can already communicate your ideas in French and understand Simple Conversations and Jokes. However, you will be taught to understand and express complex topic and ideas.

ADVANCE

At this stage you might think you have come so far to attend classes. Unfortunately, it is common for learners to get stuck because more emphasis on targeted and strategic multi-layered learning is needed to tighten up weak spots.
